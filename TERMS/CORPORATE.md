# CORPORATE TERMS
**Part of UNSL v1.0.0**

## 1. SCOPE
For businesses and organizations:
- Companies
- Non-profits
- Government agencies
- Educational institutions

## 2. ELIGIBILITY
### 2.1 Company Size
- Up to 500 employees
- Up to $50M annual revenue
- Multiple departments allowed

### 2.2 User Licensing
- Per-user or site license
- Minimum 5 users
- Maximum 100 users (standard)

## 3. PRICING
### 3.1 Per-User
- **Price:** $499/user/year
- **Volume discount:** 10% for 50+ users
- **Payment:** Annual, net 30

### 3.2 Site License
- **Price:** $24,999/year
- **Users:** Up to 100
- **Overage:** $250/user beyond 100

## 4. USAGE RIGHTS
### 4.1 Permitted
- Internal business use
- Client-facing applications
- Department-wide deployment
- Multiple environments

### 4.2 Team Collaboration
- Shared projects
- Role-based access
- Team management tools
- Centralized billing

## 5. SUPPORT
### 5.1 Included
- Priority email support (12-hour response)
- Phone support (business hours)
- Dedicated support portal
- Monthly check-ins

### 5.2 SLA
- 99.5% uptime guarantee
- 4-hour critical issue response
- 24-hour non-critical response

## 6. IMPLEMENTATION

- Technical onboarding session

### 6.2 Integration
- API access included

### 7.2 Reporting
- Quarterly usage reports
